\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a parabolic problem in the x-\/direction. 

This class solves a generic evolutionary diffusive problem in the x-\/direction with fully Dirichlet bc\textquotesingle{}s. Boundary conditions are imposed by means of a reference solution. Discretization is performed on a staggered grid and in x-\/direction only, meaning that variables are located in position LEFT and RIGHT. Linear problem is solved by means of a PETSc KSP solver, with GMRES and Jacobi preconditioner.
